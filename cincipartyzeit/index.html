APRIL

Date/Time Event/Location Description
Saturday, April 11
11am – 3pm
 Mustard Club

@ Mecklenburg Gardens 		The one and only Händlmeier Mustard Club in Cincinnati holds one of the finest events every month at Meck's consisting of a sausage buffet, pretzels, potatoes, and mustard. If you don't love mustard, leave this page immediately.
Saturday, April 18
12pm – 5pm 		Bavarian Bowling Tournament

@ Northwest Lanes 		This is one of the finest bowling tournaments to exist between the German clubs of Cincinnati. It is hosted by the Bayrischer Unterstützungsverein and it is well worth taking part in and/or hanging out with some great people.
Friday, April 24
5pm – 11pm

Saturday, April 25
12pm – 11pm 		Starkbierfest

@ Listermann Brewing Co. 		The Craft Brewer's Starkbierfest at Listermann Brewing is a must for beer lovers and those that enjoy a Bockbier. Located under a tent in a parking lot just around the corner of the brewery, this festival gets pretty busy and sometimes wet, but definitely wild.

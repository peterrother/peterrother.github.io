## Brewing Guides

### Iced Coffee

#### Requires

* Scale / Timer
* Grinder
* Pour-over brewer
* Filter
* Carafe
* Kettle
* 30g coffee

#### Steps

1. Heat water
2. Place filter in dripper
3. Rinse filter with water and dump
4. Grind and weigh coffee at 30g
5. Add 165g ice to carafe
6. Add coffee to dripper
7. Tare
8. Brew coffee to 335g
9. Swirl carafe and serve over ice
